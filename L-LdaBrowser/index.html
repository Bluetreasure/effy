About topic model

Topic modeling is an approach from the field of machine learning which seeks to characterize a large number of documents algorithmically in terms of a smaller number of themes or word clusters (referred to as topics). The result of applying the algorithm is a model: deliberately simplifying the documents, it attempts to describe their interconnections concisely and cogently. 詳見 »
